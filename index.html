Waste of Time Games

Games that trick your brain into thinking you're having fun while you progress, but you're not.

Sometimes you play again and again a game looking for new funnier strategies, and you don’t realize the game is bad.

You invesed so much time on it that you think you are closer to enjoy it well, but the mechanicks are bad designed. In the end, it’s just a game designed by someone and it doesn’t have to be well done.

You don’t want to pay for every game out there. So you choose what looks the most fun just because of its looks, and you’re stuck all night playing those games that aren’t fun to play.

Be critical of what you’re playing and look for another game if you find it’s not working for you!
